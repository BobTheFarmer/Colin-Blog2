Spanish Notes for 4 videos we watched in class

  • Video #1

    • Stem-changes are verbs that change the letters in the word when conjugated in the “boot”

      • The boot is the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 6th, boxes

    • It could be:

      • E - IE

      • E - I

      • O - UE

      • U - UE

    • To do a stem change just swap the left for the right

    • In a textbook, it would say Jugar(UE) because it’s a UE stem change

    • Change the one on the left if there is multiple
